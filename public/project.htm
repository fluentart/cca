Destination design

We want our designs to fit in with nature, instead of forcing nature to step aside, an architecture that is firmly based on the human ecotourism led them to the creation of a new kind of architecture: an architecture that is firmly based on the human experience of any specific environment.

In 1992, two brothers, Abré and Niël, set up Crafford and Crafford Architects. Their initial aim to develop eco-sensitive designs for ecotourism led them to the creation of a new kind of architecture: an architecture that is firmly based on the human experience of any specific environment; architecture that is as much an art as it is a craft; architecture that places ecology as its highest priority.
